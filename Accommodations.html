OVERVIEW

Holiday Resort, the world's largest and luxury Hotel in Malaysia, designed by Jupiter Han, is visually evocative of the mod 1960s design shot through with 21st century sensibility. Teak and chrome are the materials used in the furnishings with a sense of sleekness prevailing. Guest rooms and suites include flat-panel plasma TVs, I-Pod alarm clocks, wireless Internet access, irons and ironing boards, minibars, 400-thread-count linens, and spa amenities by Fresh. All rooms feature artwork from one of the world's most respected fashion photographers, William Teh. Blue Ribbon Sushi Bar and Grill, located off the lobby, serves Japanese cuisine and Blue Ribbon grill favorites for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Enjoy all the comforts of facility in Hotel Resort. With exclusive premium gym, outdoor pools, seaside events and more.
